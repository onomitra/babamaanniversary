# Project: Baba & Ma Anniversary Invitation

## Overview
A print-ready HTML invitation for the 50th (Golden Jubilee) wedding anniversary of **Swapan & Rita** ("Baba & Ma"), created by their children and grandchildren.

## Event Details
- **Date:** Sunday, 26th April 2026
- **Time:** 6:00 PM – 9:30 PM
- **Venue:** Kheya Hall, Anderson Club (ILSS), Rabindra Sarobor, Kolkata 700029

## Hosts
Onomitra · Ranjana · Romit & Riona (kids and grandkids)

## Files
- `anniversary_invitation.html` — original, print-only version (preserved as-is)
- `index.html` — hosted version with RSVP button and EmailJS integration (this is the GitHub Pages entry point)

## Design
- **Aesthetic:** Elegant, Indian-celebratory — deep crimson/maroon background with gold accents
- **Palette:** Background `#2a0008`, gold `#c9933a` / `#e8c46a`, cream text `#f0d898` / `#f5e4b0`
- **Fonts:** Cormorant Garamond, Cinzel Decorative, Crimson Pro (Google Fonts)
- **Ornaments:** Hand-crafted SVG lotus motifs, vine dividers, corner ornaments, double border frame
- **Size:** Letter (816×1056 px at 96dpi), print-ready with `@page` rules
- **Animations:** Staggered `fadeUp` on load (disabled on print)

## Design Principles
- Elegance is the primary criterion — preserve the maroon/gold palette and serif typography
- All decorative elements are inline SVG (no external image dependencies)
- Single self-contained HTML file for easy sharing and printing
